The dues are $70 but only $60 for anyone who joins during their final semester at UTA, and this will pay for an entire year of membership!

$55 goes to PRSA National while the rest goes to our student chapter.

The dues are due in November or March depending on which semester you join.

The dues must be paid in person at one of our meetings.

A lot of the value comes from your ability to network within the organization. Think about it this way:

Within the organization are highly-dedicated students who take their career seriously. Many of the members are involved in internships, professional networking, and entrepreneurship, and many also have mentors and peer advisors.

Therefore, by simply being around the other members, and by being active at meetings, events, and social gatherings, their connections become your connections. As a PRSSA member, you will be part of a powerful network that will keep you landing the jobs you want and the opportunities you need!

In addition, you can dramatically increase your resume value by showing your membership with PRSSA. In fact, in the communication field, your resume would be frowned upon for not having any affiliation with PRSSA at all!

You can sign up to be a member at the meetings!

Follow us on either Facebook or Twitter @PRSSAUTA to get updates on our upcoming meetings, events, and social gatherings!

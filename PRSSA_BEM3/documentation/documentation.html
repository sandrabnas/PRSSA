PRSSA SWOT Analysis

Strengths Weaknesses
  • Currently established website.
  • Website displays clear and easy to find social media links.
  • Website allows the opportunity for networking and peer to professional development
  • Website has a direct and focused area of content.

    • Maximize strengths:
    • Market experienced staff.
    • Have a get to know staff event on community calendar
    • Community classes for all ages
  • Poor mobile optimization
  • Lack of recognition around campus
  • In need of more content
  • Website tone does not stand out

    • Reduce Weaknesses:
    • Encourage clients to check the website for community events.
    • Strong presence online with new website
    • Deliver daily content regarding blog articles
Opportunities Threats
  • Membership base set in place
  • Opportunity to reach bigger audience on UTA campus
  • Financial contributions from alumni
  • Events/Conferences

    • Take advantage of opportunites:
    • Create monthly community events to attract more clients
    • Customer Appreciation day
    • Coupons for life-long clients
  • Other Organizations on campus
  • Extracurriculars
  • Part time students with full time jobs
  • Students unable to afford membership

    • Reduce impact on threats:
    • Have website set them apart from the other vets in the radius
    • Visually pleasing website
    • Honest reviews on website from previous clients
